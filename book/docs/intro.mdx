Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid robotics book!

This book provides a comprehensive guide to understanding physical AI and humanoid robotics.

## Getting Started

To begin your journey, start with the [Introduction to Physical AI](/docs/module1/introduction).

## Book Structure

This book is organized into four main modules:

1. **Foundations of Physical AI** - Core concepts and fundamentals
2. **Humanoid Robotics Engineering** - Design and control principles
3. **Intelligence, Learning & Interaction** - AI for robotics
4. **Applications, Future & Innovation** - Real-world applications and future trends
